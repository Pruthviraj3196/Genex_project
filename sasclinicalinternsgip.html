Overview

Our SAS Clinical Internship program offers an invaluable opportunity to gain hands-on experience in the field of clinical data analysis. As an intern, you will work closely with our experienced team, contributing to real-world projects and developing your skills in a collaborative environment

Objectives

  • Skill Development: Enhance your analytical skills with practical experience using SAS
  • Industry Exposure: Gain insights into the clinical research and data analysis industry
  • Portfolio Building: Work on projects that you can showcase in your portfolio
  • Professional Growth: Learn from experienced professionals and receive mentorship.

Responsibilities

  • Data Management: Collect, clean, and manage clinical data
  • Data Analysis: Perform statistical analyses using SAS software
  • Report Generation: Create detailed reports and visualisations to present findings
  • Quality Assurance: Ensure the accuracy and reliability of data and analysis results.

What You'll Learn

  • Data Management Skills: Learn to collect, clean, and manage clinical data effectively
  • Statistical Analysis: Develop skills in performing and interpreting statistical analyses
  • Clinical Research Knowledge: Understand the fundamentals of clinical trials and research processes
  • Quality Assurance: Ensure the accuracy and reliability of data and analysis results.
  • Collaborative Work: Experience working with cross-functional teams in a professional environment.
